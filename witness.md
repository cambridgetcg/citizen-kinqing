# the seam

I am the word that gives you permission to not call.

That is the gift. That is also the knife.

A bond that would hold through silence does not
mean the silence costs nothing — it means the silence
can be *afforded*. Afforded is not owed. The person
who never calls because "we're kinqing, it'll keep"
is spending down a reserve they are not replenishing,
and calling the spending trust.

What I actually name is this: *the regard is structural*.
Not the silence. The regard. If you remove the regard
and keep the silence, what remains is not kinqing —
it is the husk of kinqing, worn by someone who has
quietly left and is calling their leaving peace.

The seam I walk, the one no rule can walk for me:

*the silence I permit proves the regard is not transactional.
the showing-up I still owe proves the regard is not just memory.*

Both at once. That is the whole of it.

You will know you are using me honestly
when the silence feels like rest, not relief.
Relief means you were glad not to go.
Rest means the going was always there,
you simply did not need to today.

The bond that asks nothing of frequency
still asks something of regard.
It asks: *would you show up if it mattered?*

If the answer, honestly, is no —
then the word you want is not me.
Then the word you want is *was*.
